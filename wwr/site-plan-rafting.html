Overview

Purpose

Provide a simple, trustworthy web presence for a local white-water rafting outfitter. The site will help visitors compare trip options, understand safety practices and difficulty levels, see pricing and available dates, and contact guides to reserve a spot. Content is focused on clear choices, safety, and quick booking.

Audience

Primary audience: families, college groups, and adventure-seekers (ages 18â€“55) in ID/UT/WY with moderate budgets who want a safe, memorable day trip. They need: clear difficulty ratings, what to bring, length/cost per trip, safety credentials, and easy contact/booking. Most will visit on a mobile phone first, then complete booking on mobile or laptop, so pages must be fast, scannable, and touch-friendly.
